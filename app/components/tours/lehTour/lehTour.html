Stok La Trek

This is one of the beautiful and easy accessible trek in Ladakh. The trek offers spectacular views of surrounding mountains and Indus valley.

Embarking on this trek is quite a thrilling experience, it has moderate difficulty level. One of the best moments on the trek is trekking to Rumbak village as the valley suddenly opens up with snowcapped mountains in the background which is a great place to view Ladakh flora and fauna.

Day 1 :
Reach leh, flying over some of the most spectacular scenery over the upper Himalayas. It is essential to spend the day acclimatizing to the rarer air and higher altitude in Leh.
Day 2:
Spend the day at leisure, exploring the main market, and orienting yourself to the bustle of the small Leh town.
Day 3:
Drive to Zingchen, and hour’s drive from Leh. From there, we walk up gradually to the village of Rumbak(3900m), the snow leopard capital of the world.

Trek time: 3-4 hours

Day 4:
The high point of your trek in many ways, we climb the Stok La (4900m), the highest point of this trek. We descend sharply to Changma, where we camp for the night.

Trek time: 6-7 hours

Day 5:
An easy day of walking, descend into Stok village, from where your car will come and pick you up.

Trek time: 2-3 hours

Day 6:
Assisted transfer to the airport.

Markha Valley

Markha Valley is one of the most famous treks of Ladakh Region. It is an amazing trip to get introduced with magical and remote Buddhist Kingdom of Ladakh. While trekking on through this trek tourists passes through beautiful Buddhist Monasteries, Mountain Villages, High altitude pastures of Nimaling and High altitude peak Kangyatse.
